What is Quizard?

Origins

Quizzard was created to fulfil the requirements for the second project of CITS5505: Agile Web Development.

Purpose

The purpose of Quizzard is to provide an online, interactive quiz platform available for everyone!

Features of Quizard

Navigation

Use the navigation bar at the top to navigate between taking a quiz, making a quiz and everything in between!.

You can createa quiz with multi-choice, voting and free-form text questions, or any combination of those three.
